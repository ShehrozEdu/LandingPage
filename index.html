3 Steps to Optimize Private Equity Executive
Recruitment

So, how can you optimize your private equity firm’s executive recruitment strategy to maximize your time and your ROI? Here are three steps to help:

1. Find the right executive recruitment firm to meet your private equity firm’s specific needs.

Many executive recruitment companies will do their best to find top-notch, seasoned executives to fill the myriad of opportunities they receive from various private equity firms. They might have a rolodex of seasoned executives from which they’re pulling or they might be scouring LinkedIn for new recruitment leads. Either way, their tactics are timely and, especially for private equity firms, their tactics are costly.

With GeniusMesh, however, our tactics utilize the best of AI and People Intelligence to ensure we find you the RightFit™ in record time. How does GeniusMesh manage to outperform other executive recruiters? We have an engaged global network of 12,000+ Executive MBAs from the leading 30+ business schools with an average of 18+ years of operational experience.

Our record speaks for itself:

  • 60% faster overall hiring time
  • 97% retention for our hires
  • 4x faster pipeline creation
  • 6x faster hiring with 1-1 chat
  • 90% improvement in cultural alignment

A few recent examples of roles we’ve filled include:

  • Chief Digital Officer of a $46B company (US, Singapore) in four days
  • Head of Sales for a $10M company (North America) in twelve days
  • EVP Business Transformation for a $16B company (LATM) in two days
  • Chief Revenue Officer for a $5B company (Global) in five days

With GeniusMesh, you gain access to our community of eager and experienced executives and are able to hold 1:1 conversations with potential candidates prior to a formal interview. We also ensure that whomever you hire is a professional, experiential, and cultural fit for your company by utilizing our proprietary technology for a 360 degree review of the candidate.

We maximize efficiency to maximize your ROI when it comes to private equity executive recruitment. No matter the niche, GeniusMesh has the right fit for your company.

2. Find the right person for your professional, experiential, and cultural needs.

Getting access to a talent pool of diverse professionals with experience in more than 50 industries and the mindset of specialized generalists can give PE & VC firms a dramatic boost to their growth strategies. With the support of highly skilled executive search professionals and a proprietary AI-powered platform, PE & VC professionals can count on the effectiveness and timeliness of GeniusMesh.

Our proprietary technology helps us to achieve our 94% retention rate and will optimize your private equity firm’s ROI. Developed in association with a GeniusMesh advisor & Wharton professor with a PhD in Organizational Psychology, our assessments create fully encompassing candidate and company profiles to best establish cultural alignment, professional expertise, and improved retention rates.

Our assessment enables candidates to not only learn their strengths and weaknesses, but learn from their strengths & weaknesses. Each candidate receives detailed steps to help maximize their strengths and improve their weaknesses. Our assessment also illustrates specific career trajectories and shows individuals the best path based on their experience, psychological profile, and cultural alignment.

3. Find a candidate who avoids silo solutions to maximize your firm’s ROI in your private equity executive recruitment search.

Especially in the PE world, finding and hiring the right person is pivotal to a portfolio company’s ultimate success or subpar performance. Portfolio company performance is predicated upon the executive team that is able to manage, collaborate, solve, and execute its directives with agility. There is no room for “silo solution” leaders. Portcos thrive when their executive teams function as specialized generalists and collaborate seamlessly to achieve their desired ROI for the private equity parent firms.

At GeniusMesh, we do not have any “silo solution” leaders; we only have a vast and engaged community of global thought leaders and “specialized generalists.” Together with laser-focused execution, our EMBAs leverage their specialized generalist nature to avoid silo solutions and utilize their broad-based mindset to identify problems and execute solutions. This type of professional prowess is crucial for the quick turnarounds needed in many private equity strategies.

Moreover, the environment of EMBA Programs mimics the environment that PE & VC professionals will experience with a newly acquired portfolio company’s management team. In both cases, individuals with varied backgrounds are “thrown together” to address issues in a timely manner to achieve a common goal. An EMBA degree is a strong indicator an individual will be able to rise to the challenge and help unlock the intrinsic value of a portfolio company.

We also offer executive coaching services to mitigate any lingering silo solution executives and foster the intrinsic value of your portfolio company. Companies that invest in executive coaching services often see a 7x growth in their ROI because of their team’s improved alignment towards the common goal.

It’s time to unlock your portfolio companies’ intrinsic value by unlocking the power of private equity executive recruitment with GeniusMesh. With pre-vetted candidates in our world-class network of business executives and industry experts, you can make the most of your time and resources and find the best leaders for your business with GeniusMesh.
